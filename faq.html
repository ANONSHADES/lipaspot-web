How does Lipaspot confirm M-Pesa payments? â–¶
When a customer makes a payment to your business's M-Pesa till, the Lipaspot software, which is configured on a PDQ machine, captures the payment confirmation message. It then instantly broadcasts this message to the relevant attendants, waiters, or cashiers on their mobile devices, ensuring on-the-spot verification and eliminating manual checks.
How does the system help staff claim transactions? â–¶
Our system allows the staff member who served the customer to "claim" the transaction directly on their phone. This simple action links the specific M-Pesa payment to the individual employee, ensuring accurate tracking of who handled which sale.
